面料与科技 Fabrics & Technology

科技面料 + 精准裁剪 = 理想塑形效果 Technical fabric + precision cutting = ideal shaping results

🧶 高弹锦纶+氨纶High-Recovery Nylon + Spandex

采用意大利进口高弹纱线，80%锦纶提供强度与平整表面，20%氨纶提供卓越弹力。面料回复率≥98%，经过50次机洗后压力损耗不超过8%。 Italian high-recovery yarns: 80% Nylon for strength and smooth surface, 20% Spandex for exceptional stretch. Recovery rate ≥98%. Pressure loss ≤8% after 50 machine washes.

📐 梯度压力技术Graduated Compression

腰部区域压力最大（约25mmHg），向下逐渐递减至大腿区域（约15mmHg）。梯度差促进血液循环，久穿不麻木，安全舒适。 Highest compression at waist (~25mmHg), gradually decreasing to thighs (~15mmHg). The gradient promotes blood circulation, safe for extended wear without numbness.

🪡 无缝一体编织Seamless Circular Knit

全件无缝编织工艺，无侧缝、无拼接、无硬质接缝。不同压力区域在同一编织过程中完成，过渡自然平滑。 Fully seamless circular knitting — no side seams, no panels, no rigid joints. Different compression zones are knit in a single process for smooth, natural transitions.
