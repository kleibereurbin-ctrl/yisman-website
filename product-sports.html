专利吸湿排汗纤维结构，汗水通过毛细效应被快速吸收并扩散至面料外层蒸发。保持皮肤表面干爽，运动舒适度提升300%。 Patented moisture-wicking fiber structure uses capillary action to rapidly absorb and spread sweat to the outer layer for evaporation. Keeps skin dry — 300% improvement in workout comfort.
