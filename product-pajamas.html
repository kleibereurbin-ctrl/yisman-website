3

批量生产Bulk Production

面料预缩处理后裁剪缝制。每套睡衣经过三道检验：面料检验、半成品检验、成品检验。AQL 2.5标准。 Fabric pre-shrunk before cutting and sewing. Three inspection stages: fabric, work-in-progress, finished goods. AQL 2.5 standard.

4

包装发运Packaging & Shipping

按客户要求包装（散装或礼盒装），打包后安排物流。提供实时物流追踪，确保安全准时到达。 Packaged per client requirements (bulk or gift box), then shipped with real-time tracking for safe and on-time delivery.
