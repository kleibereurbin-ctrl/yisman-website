面料与工艺 Materials & Craftsmanship

🌾 100% 精梳棉100% Combed Cotton

采用新疆阿克苏长绒棉，精梳工艺剔除15%以下的短纤维。面料支数40-60支，透气性极佳，吸湿率8%-10%，是最经典的天然内衣面料。不易起球，不易变形。 Xinjiang Aksu long-staple cotton, combed to remove fibers under 15mm. 40-60s count. Excellent breathability with 8-10% moisture regain. The classic natural underwear fabric — resists pilling and deformation.

🌿 兰精莫代尔Lenzing Modal®

奥地利兰精公司生产的高湿模量纤维素纤维。生态环保，可自然降解。手感如丝般柔滑，比棉吸水性高50%，是高端内裤的首选面料。建议搭配5%氨纶以获得更好的弹力回复。 High-wet-modulus cellulose fiber from Lenzing, Austria. Eco-friendly and biodegradable. Silky touch with 50% higher absorbency than cotton — the premium choice. Recommended with 5% spandex for elasticity.

🔗 无缝针织技术Seamless Knit Technology

无缝一片式内裤，没有侧缝线，穿着无痕更隐形。特别适合紧身裤和包臀裙穿搭。采用意大利无缝机生产，效率高、品质稳定。 Seamless one-piece panties with zero side seams — completely invisible under leggings and bodycon skirts. Made on Italian seamless machines for high efficiency and consistent quality.
